Importance of Time Management

Time management helps us stay organized and complete our tasks on time. When we plan properly, we feel less stressed and more confident. Good time management also allows us to balance studies, rest, and hobbies.

Challenges

Many people struggle with distractions and poor planning. However, with small habits like making a to-do list or avoiding unnecessary phone use, these challenges can be handled easily.

Simple Planning Tips

Create small goals, take short breaks, and complete one task at a time. These small steps help build discipline and improve focus.
